PBC Annual Cookie Project

A Community Powered Cookie Drive

The Annual Cookie Project first took place in 1987 in the San Lorenzo Valley Community in Santa Cruz, California. It has now made its way all the way to the east coast of South Florida with the First Annual Cookie Project taking place in Palm Beach County in 2019 in collaboration with The Soup Kitchen Of Boynton Beach.

Read More

We had almost 10,000 cookie baked and donated which were packaged in variety packs of 8 cookies making a total of 1126 holiday cookie bags. Hustle Butter donated 1200 packets of hot chocolate mix to be included in each cookie bag.

Our volunteers passed out hundreds of bags of cookies at The Soup Kitchen Of Boynton Beach at their Santa Day event on December 21st, 2019. We were also able to deliver cookies to 30 families with special needs children, 30 emergency workers working on xmas (thank you to the JCC), 30 seniors living in a home, approximately 45 homeless people, 86 families sponsored by St. Marks Catholic Church, as well as dozens of other families and individuals in need this holiday season.

Palm Beach County wants to challenge any county in 2020, can you bake and donate more cookies than us next year? We will be bringing the heat as this year was only planned in 2 weeks time. We plan to double our cookie donations next year. Are you in?
